> **Instructions for maintainers:** This report is completed by a HWSETA-appointed External Moderator after conducting moderation of a learner cohort's assessment packages. Complete all sections and replace every placeholder in `UPPER_SNAKE_CASE` before submitting to HWSETA and the institution. Retain a signed copy in institutional records for the period required by HWSETA (minimum five years).

**Severity Definitions:**

| Severity | Definition |
|---------|-----------|
| **Critical** | A fundamental failure that invalidates assessment outcomes or compromises learner rights. Immediate suspension of further assessments in the affected area may be required pending corrective action. |
| **Major** | A significant deficiency that materially affects the quality, fairness, or validity of the assessment. Corrective action must be completed before results are submitted to HWSETA. |
| **Minor** | A deficiency that does not materially affect assessment outcomes but must be addressed to maintain compliance. May be resolved at the next moderation cycle. |
