# Accreditation Evidence Alignment Table – SAQA/HWSETA

This document maps the required accreditation evidence to the relevant HWSETA criteria for both NQF Level 4 (Peer Counseling) and NQF Level 5 (Facilitators) qualifications. Use it alongside the assessment and moderation templates to confirm that all evidence portfolios are complete before submission.

---

## Alignment Summary

- **Dual-level coverage:** The evidence framework spans NQF Level 4 (Peer Counseling) and NQF Level 5 (Facilitators), ensuring the organisation can seek accreditation at both qualification levels simultaneously.
- **Template cross-referencing:** Every evidence item maps to a named template in this folder, eliminating ambiguity during PoE compilation and ETQA audits.
- **Shared artefacts:** Certain documents (e.g., Programme Design Document, Results Schedule, ETQA Communication File) serve both levels; maintain a single master copy and reference it from each PoE.
- **Assessor/moderator separation:** All assessment and moderation roles must remain independent at both levels; confirm role separation is documented before submission.
- **Compliance windows:** HWSETA reporting cycles typically open in March and September; verify current dates on the HWSETA website and schedule evidence finalisation at least four weeks prior.
- **Record retention:** All accreditation evidence must be retained for a minimum of five years in line with SAQA and POPIA requirements.

## Deployment Notes

The templates in this folder should be populated by the designated quality assurance (QA) officer before each intake cycle. Completed and verified evidence portfolios must be stored in the organisation's document management system and backed up off-site. Any amendments to unit standards or HWSETA accreditation requirements should trigger an immediate review of this alignment table. Please raise a repository issue or contact the QA lead if updates are required.
