## Assessment Purpose and Approach

This assessment guide covers both **formative** (in-module) and **summative** (end-of-module) assessment for the above UTC module. All tasks are mapped to specific outcomes of the relevant HWSETA unit standards and UTC module competencies. Assessment is competency-based: learners are rated **Competent (C)** or **Not Yet Competent (NYC)** on each outcome, with marks provided for formative tasks to give developmental feedback.

### Overall Competence Ruling

A learner is deemed **Competent** if all learning outcomes are rated C. A learner with one or more NYC outcomes must be given remediation and reassessment opportunity before a final ruling is made.

---

## Reassessment Policy

Learners who receive a NYC ruling are entitled to one reassessment opportunity within 30 days. The reassessor must use an equivalent but different task where possible.
