# Accreditation Evidence Alignment Table – Outpatient Treatment Curriculum (UTC)

This document maps the required accreditation evidence to the relevant HWSETA criteria for NQF Level 4 (Peer Counseling in Substance Abuse) and NQF Level 5 (Substance Abuse Counselling Practitioner) qualifications in the outpatient treatment context. Evidence items align to both HWSETA unit standards and Universal Treatment Curriculum (UTC) modules.

## Alignment Summary

- **Dual-level coverage:** The evidence framework spans NQF Level 4 (Peer Counseling) and NQF Level 5 (Substance Abuse Counselling Practitioner), ensuring the organisation can seek accreditation at both qualification levels simultaneously.
- **UTC integration:** Each accreditation criterion is explicitly linked to UTC modules so that evidence collection occurs naturally during curriculum delivery rather than as a separate compliance activity.
- **Template cross-referencing:** Every evidence item maps to a named template in the assessment and facilitator-resources folders, eliminating ambiguity during PoE compilation and ETQA audits.
- **Outpatient placement hours:** Both NQF levels require documented outpatient placement hours; confirm minimum hours against current unit standard requirements before each intake.
- **Assessor/moderator separation:** All assessment and moderation roles must remain independent at both levels; confirm role separation is documented before submission.
- **Compliance windows:** HWSETA reporting cycles typically open in March and September; verify current dates on the HWSETA website and schedule evidence finalisation at least four weeks prior.
- **Record retention:** All accreditation evidence must be retained for a minimum of five years in line with SAQA and POPIA requirements.

## Deployment Notes

The templates in this folder should be populated by the designated quality assurance (QA) officer before each intake cycle. Completed and verified evidence portfolios must be stored in the organisation's document management system and backed up off-site. Any amendments to unit standards, HWSETA accreditation requirements, or UTC module content should trigger an immediate review of this alignment table. Please raise a repository issue or contact the QA lead if updates are required.
