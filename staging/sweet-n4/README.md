# SWEET-N4 – SAQA/HWSETA NQF Level 4 Compliance Package

**SWEET-N4** (Substance Wellness Education & Employment Training – NQF Level 4) is a comprehensive compliance documentation package for the **HWSETA Peer Counseling in Substance Abuse** qualification (NQF Level 4).

This staging folder contains all templates, forms, and evidence alignment documents required for SAQA/HWSETA accreditation and learner assessment.

---

## Document Structure

### 1. Programme Design
- Programme Design Document (aligned to US 117151, US 110009)
- Curriculum Overview
- Learning Pathway Map

### 2. Assessment
- Assessment Guide Template (per unit standard)
- Formative Assessment Pack
- Summative Assessment Guide
- Marking Memoranda

### 3. Learner Resources
- Learner Registration Form
- Portfolio of Evidence (PoE) Cover Sheet
- PoE Evidence Checklist
- Learner Guide Template

### 4. Moderation
- Internal Moderation Report Template
- Pre-Assessment Moderation Checklist
- Post-Assessment Moderation Report

### 5. Workplace Evidence
- Workplace Logbook Template
- Supervisor Sign-off Form
- Practical Hours Verification

### 6. Policies
- Appeals and Grievances Policy
- Assessment Policy
- Learner Code of Conduct
- POPIA Consent and Data Protection

### 7. Reporting
- Results Schedule Template
- Statement of Results
- HWSETA Upload Checklist
- ETQA Communication Log

---

## Unit Standards Covered

**Primary Unit Standards:**
- **US 117151** – Demonstrate knowledge of substance abuse and dependency
- **US 110009** – Facilitate a peer counseling session in substance abuse

**Additional Electives:**
- US 116932 – Apply basic communication skills in a helping relationship
- US 119567 – Facilitate group work sessions
- US 263498 – Demonstrate understanding of HIV/AIDS in a health and social context

## Accreditation Requirements

All documentation in this package aligns with:
- SAQA Assessment Quality Partner (AQP) requirements
- HWSETA Skills Development Provider (SDP) accreditation criteria
- NQF Level 4 assessment standards
- QCTO Assessment Quality Framework

## Usage Instructions

1. **Populate Templates:** Complete all placeholders in each template with your organisation details
2. **Customise Content:** Adapt learning materials to your specific delivery context
3. **Quality Assurance:** Review all documents with your internal QA officer before learner intake
4. **Evidence Collection:** Maintain a master evidence file for each learner, cross-referenced to the PoE checklist
5. **Moderation:** Ensure assessor/moderator independence and complete moderation before submitting results
6. **ETQA Submission:** Submit completed evidence portfolios to HWSETA within the designated reporting window

---

## Compliance Checklist

Before submitting to HWSETA, verify:
- [ ] Programme design document maps to all unit standard outcomes
- [ ] Assessors hold NQF Level 5 or higher and are HWSETA-registered
- [ ] Assessment guides include detailed marking memoranda
- [ ] Learner PoE contains authentic, current, and sufficient evidence
- [ ] Workplace logbooks meet minimum practical hours requirement
- [ ] Internal moderation completed by independent, registered moderator
- [ ] Learner registration forms include POPIA consent
- [ ] Results schedule accurately reflects learner achievements
- [ ] Appeals policy communicated to learners at intake
- [ ] All ETQA correspondence archived for 5+ years
